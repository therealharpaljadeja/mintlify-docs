The goals of this attestation protocol are to increase adoption of identity mappings, and encourage network effects of shared cross-application identity attestations. To this end, we have made it possible for anyone to permissionlessly become an issuer of attestations.

## Architecture

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="ODIS" icon="server" iconType="regular" href="/protocol/architecture/odis">
  The Oblivious Decentralized Identifier Service (ODIS) allows for privacy preserving identifiers by implementing a rate limited oblivious pseudorandom function (OPRF).
</Card>

<Card title="FederatedAttestations.sol" icon="file-contract" iconType="regular" href="/protocol/architecture/federatedattestations">
  FederatedAttestations.sol is the onchain contract that acts a registry of attestations. All the mappings of obfuscated identifiers to their wallet address is stored here.
</Card>

</CardGroup>
